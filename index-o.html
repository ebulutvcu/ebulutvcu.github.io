Our Research Interests

  • Mobile computing: wireless networks, cyber-physical systems, Internet of Things (IoT), crowd-sensing, social networks, wireless charging;
  • Security: cyber security, network security, privacy and trust;
  • Cellular (3G/4G) network: mobile packet core, cloud, mobile data offloading;

One Ph.D. position is currently available. Master's degree is required. Candidates with interests in wireless and mobile computing are encouraged to apply. Please email your CV (including GPA, TOEFL, and GRE scores (with Q>160)) to Dr. Bulut at ebulut [at] vcu [dot] edu together with a discussion/improvement ideas on one of our papers.

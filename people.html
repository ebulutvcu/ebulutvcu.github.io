Bio

Eyuphan Bulut is an Associate Professor at Computer Science Department of Virginia Commonwealth University (VCU). Before he joined VCU, he was working for Cisco Systems as a senior engineer in Mobile Internet Technology Group (MITG). He received his Ph.D. degree in computer science department of Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute (RPI) in 2011. Dr. Bulut and his team is the recipient of several awards including VCU Presidential Quest Fund, IEEE International Conference on Pervasive Computing and Communications (PerCom 2020) best demo award, and Air Force Research Lab (AFRL) Summer Faculty Fellowship. Dr. Bulut is on the Editorial Board of Elsevier Ad Hoc Networks and IEEE Access. CV.
